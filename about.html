About Me

I am an artist based out of St Louis who has a real loyalty to his home town. I graduated in 2007 from The Kansas City Art Institute with a BFA in Animation. I am currently attending BYUI pursuing a degree in web development, while strengthening my skills in Illustrator, Photoshop, and Indesign at my job as a Pre Flight Tech. I plan on leaving the print world in pursuit of using my creative skills to create web technology with an innovative team that is focused on making the world a better place.
